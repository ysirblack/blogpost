Surah Al-Qiyamah is the 75th Surah of the Qur'an you can check it here

- In the Quran, Allah poses a rhetorical question to those who deny the Day of Judgment and the power of Allah to resurrect the dead. Allah emphasizes His immense power by declaring that He is capable of restoring even the minutest detail, such as fingerprints, which are unique to each individual, after death. This serves as a powerful reminder of Allah's infinite ability to resurrect the dead and His ultimate authority over all creation.

- Indeed, the fact that fingerprints are unique and can be used for identification was not known during the time when the Quran was revealed. It is a remarkable scientific discovery that was not established until many centuries later. The Quran's assertion of Allah's ability to restore such a detail is a testament to its timeless relevance and divine origin. It is a sign of Allah's limitless knowledge and power, and a reminder that the Quran is not only a source of guidance but also a book of miraculous revelations.
